TravelByte: because you forgot this was important info
Welcome to TravelByte, a user-friendly app to assist your travel plans. Whether you are planning for a vacation, or simply looking up travel information for fun, we are here for you! So sit back and get ready to take a Byte of your travel plan :)

Currency Exchange

The current exchange rate from the selected country compared to U.S. Dollars

Big Mac Index

This is a purchasing power comparison. How many Big Macs you can buy compared to the U.S. cost. The higher index number, the further your money will go in this country.

Temperature

The average temperature in the selected city of the month you wish to travel.

Monthly Rainfall

The average monthly rainfall in the selected city and month of the month you wish to travel.
